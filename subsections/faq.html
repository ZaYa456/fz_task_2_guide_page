FAQ & Troubleshooting

How do I reset my password? ▼
You can reset your password from the Profile section in your dashboard. Click on "Update Password" and follow the prompts. If you're locked out, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to receive a reset email.
Why aren't my menu changes showing on the public page? ▼
Changes may take a few moments to appear. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If issues persist, check that you've saved your changes in the dashboard and that the items are marked as "available" or "active".
Can I have multiple users managing the dashboard? ▼
Yes! You can add multiple team members from the Users section. Each user gets their own login credentials, and you can assign different permission levels to control what each user can access and modify.
How do I upload multiple products at once? ▼
Use the Import Excel feature in the Products section. First, download the template to see the correct format. Fill in your product information, save the file, and then upload it. This is much faster than adding items one by one.
What image formats and sizes are recommended? ▼
For product images, use JPG or PNG format, ideally 800x800 pixels or larger. For your logo, 500x500 pixels works well. The cover photo should be around 1920x600 pixels. Keep file sizes under 2MB for faster loading.
How do customers place orders? ▼
Customers browse your menu, add items to their cart, and then submit their order via WhatsApp. The order details are sent directly to your WhatsApp Business number, where you can confirm and coordinate delivery or pickup.
Can I temporarily hide items without deleting them? ▼
Yes! When editing a product, you can mark it as unavailable or inactive. This keeps it in your database but hides it from customers. You can easily make it visible again when needed.
How do I change my subscription plan? ▼
Go to the Billing section in your dashboard. You'll see your current plan and options to upgrade or downgrade. Changes typically take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What happens if my subscription expires? ▼
If your subscription expires, your public menu will no longer be accessible to customers. Your data remains safe, and you can reactivate your account at any time by renewing your subscription in the Billing section.
Can I customize the look of my menu page? ▼
Yes! In the Settings section, you can upload your logo and cover photo, set brand colors, and customize various visual elements to match your restaurant's identity.
Is there a limit to how many products I can add? ▼
Limits depend on your subscription plan. Check your plan details in the Billing section. If you need to add more products, consider upgrading to a higher tier.
Who can I contact for additional support? ▼
If you need help beyond this guide, you can contact our support team through the contact information provided in your dashboard, or check for a support/help section within the platform for live chat or email support.
